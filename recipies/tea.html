Tea

Description

Your parents will MAKE you make this. Or your relatives. So, even if you don't drink its a pretty handy recipie to know

It might be useful when coffee ain't available too.

Ingredients

  • Tea, 3 teaspoon
  • Water, 3 cups
  • Sugar (according to preferance. A teaspoon is advisable.
  • Milk, half a cup

Steps

  1. Put some water in a cylindrical pan
  2. Put stove on full heat.
  3. Put the pan on the stove.
  4. Put tea in water.
  5. When the water boils add some milk.
  6. When the mixture boils, add some sugar to make the "thing" tea.
  7. Serve in cups
