Maggi

Description

This is very simple and easy to implement maggi recipie. It can be done by anyone who has access to a packet of maggi and can play with the risk of burning their hands off.

Since its so easy to follow and works in under 5 minutes, its basically THE recipie children should start with (and the recipie most adults stuck with)

  • A packet of maggi
  • Water
  1. Open your stove and put enough water in it to soak 2 packets worth of maggi in
  2. Turn on the gas on full setting and wait for the water to boil
  3. When the water boils, put the gas on medium seeting
  4. Put maggi masala in the water
  5. Add the maggi in the pan
  6. If you are a expert you can add different spices, condiments and masalas to taste
  7. Occasionally stir and wait for the water to boil off according to prefrences
  8. Serve hot
